Stephanie's Coffee

Description

It may seem like a plain cup of coffee, but I make it for my wife everyday before I go to work.

It is an expression of my love

Ingredients

  • coffee
  • creamer
  • Benefiber
  • sweetener

Steps

  1. 4 Tbsp creamer
  2. 1 Tbsp Benefiber
  3. 4-5 packets of sweetener
  4. of course, coffee
  5. stir and serve
