Alabama Fire Crackers

Description

I originally got this recipe from a nurse I used to work with. I found the name on Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 1 box of Whole grain saltine crackers
  • 10 oz of canola oil
  • 4 Tbsp or 1 packet of Hidden Valley Ranch Mix powder
  • 1-3 Tbsp of crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 Tbsp of garlic powder

Steps

  1. Place crackers in a large mixing bowl
  2. Put all of the other ingredients into a mixing cup
  3. Mix vigorously
  4. Pour mixture over the crackers
  5. Close the lid and gently mix
  6. Let sit 15 minutes
  7. Turn and agitate
  8. Repeat 3 times, total 1 hour

You can eat them out of the container or store in a zip-loc bag

They do not last long
